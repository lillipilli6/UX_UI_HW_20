Hi, I'm Lisa

UX/UI Designer in Melbourne, Australia.

I am an Innovative and empathetic creative who is passionate about improving the lives of others through design.

I have extensive experience as a senior designer in advertising and the, corporate and education sectors spanning 10 years. Experience has given me a deep understanding of disparate demographics, ages, cultures and diversities and abilities.

My expertise lies in researching and identifying user needs, business objectives, available resources and coordinating the needs of both the user and the business to achieve a balanced optimum solution. I am flexible, accustomed to working in flux and can easily adapt by changing designs, needs and priorities with merging user insights.
